# Decision Records (Why They Exist)

**Status**: Stable
**Date**: 2026-01-13
**Purpose**: Explains why AgentOS uses decision records to preserve rationale (Explanation).

---

## 1. Why decision records exist
- Behavioral changes without rationale cause drift.
- Decisions capture tradeoffs so future changes can reason against prior constraints.

## 2. Where they live
- Decision records live in `docs/explanation/agentos/decisions/`.
- The required format is defined in `docs/reference/agentos/decision-record-format.md`.
