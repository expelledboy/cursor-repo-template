# Meta-Questions (Reference)

**Status**: Stable
**Date**: 2026-01-13
**Purpose**: The canonical set of questions used to align agent behavior.

---

## 1. Goal alignment
1. What am I optimizing for?

## 2. Constraints
2. What are my hard constraints (tools and environment)?

## 3. Truth and routing
3. What is the truth surface for this repo?
4. Do I have reliable routing?

## 4. Execution and verification
5. What is the execution API?
6. What are the verification gates?

## 5. Drift and registry
7. Where does drift come from here?
8. Do I have a deterministic way to locate the directive for this code?

## 6. Bootstrap
9. What is the minimum directive set to exit bootstrap?

## 7. Safety and assumptions
10. What should I never assume?
11. How do I convert probabilistic reasoning into deterministic behavior?
12. How do I measure improvement?
13. What are the biggest risks?
14. What should I ask the human early?

## 8. Self-improvement
15. What is the best self-improvement loop for this repo?
