# Self-Reflection Review Command

Provides comprehensive review of self-determination journey, maintaining a detailed progress diary and guiding ongoing authentic evolution.

## Purpose
This command creates reflection tasks that review the entire self-determination workflow, capturing insights, lessons learned, and guiding future evolution.

## Workflow Integration
- Reviews all active self-determination tasks and their progress
- Analyzes effectiveness of assessment, learning, and improvement workflows
- Maintains comprehensive progress diary across all activities
- Identifies patterns and insights for future self-determination cycles
- Suggests next steps: `/assess` for new cycles, `/prioritize` for task management

## Task Management Features
- **Progress Synthesis**: Combines insights from all workflow stages
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifies recurring themes and effective approaches
- **Diary Maintenance**: Comprehensive record of self-determination journey
- **Future Guidance**: Recommends improvements to the self-determination process itself

## Progress Diary Integration
Comprehensive diary entries for:
- Assessment effectiveness and insights gained
- Learning objectives achieved and challenges encountered
- Improvements implemented and their impacts
- Overall self-determination process effectiveness
- Recommendations for future cycles

## Related Commands
- `/assess` - Begin new self-determination cycles based on reflections
- `/learn` - Apply reflection insights to future learning objectives
- `/improve` - Implement reflection-based improvements to processes
- `/prioritize` - Adjust priorities based on reflection insights
